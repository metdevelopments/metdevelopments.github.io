About MET DEVELOPMENTS LTD

Residential building specialists based in Leigh-on-Sea.

MET DEVELOPMENTS LTD delivers residential construction projects with a practical and disciplined approach. Our work covers new-build homes, structural extensions, and small to mid-scale development schemes. Each project is managed with attention to structural performance, regulatory compliance, and realistic construction timelines.

We coordinate closely with architects, engineers, and planning consultants to ensure that every stage meets UK building standards. Our team focuses on efficient site management, controlled procurement, and transparent communication throughout the build cycle.

We prioritise durable materials, energy-conscious solutions, and layouts designed for everyday use. The objective is not only to complete construction, but to deliver properties that remain functional and valuable over time.

From initial feasibility to final handover, projects are delivered with documented milestones and clear reporting. Clients receive predictable progress and controlled execution rather than uncertainty.

Construction Services

Focused residential solutions.

New Build Homes

Complete residential construction from groundworks through to final finishes. Structural safety, insulation performance, and build quality are prioritised at every stage. Projects are delivered in accordance with current UK regulations and inspection requirements.

Residential Development Projects

Support for small and medium housing developments including planning coordination and build delivery. Cost control and programme discipline ensure predictable outcomes. Developments are structured to maximise long-term property value.

Extensions and Conversions

Our Mission and Standards

What drives our work.

Our mission is to deliver residential construction that balances design quality with practical performance. We focus on efficient building methods that reduce delays and unnecessary cost escalation.

Energy efficiency is considered at the planning stage through insulation strategy, ventilation performance, and material selection. This supports lower long-term operating costs for property owners.

We aim to maintain predictable delivery through detailed scheduling, supplier planning, and structured site supervision. Quality checks are conducted at key build stages rather than only at completion.

All decisions are guided by long-term durability, safety, and usability rather than short-term visual appeal alone.

How We Deliver Projects

Structured execution.

Stage 1 – Assessment

Initial discussion, site evaluation, scope definition, and feasibility review including planning and budget considerations.

Stage 2 – Planning

Programme development, procurement scheduling, contractor coordination, and regulatory preparation before construction begins.

Stage 3 – Construction and Handover

Build execution with staged inspections, quality control, and final documentation upon completion.
